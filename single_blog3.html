Softwear skills are on the rise in the corporate industry which appeals to students

Rakibul Islam

There are a number of basic computer skills that employers expect, as well as some advanced skills that will improve chances of getting hired. These are some of the most important computer skills for resume.
Microsoft Word:
Microsoft Word is one of the most ubiquitous pieces of computer software and the majority of people are familiar with it in some way. The average person has the skills to create a simple word document, but having a more extensive knowledge of Microsoft Word will improve your chances of getting hired. Knowing how to do things like insert images and tables, create document templates and take advantage of the more complex features of Microsoft Word will set you apart from the competition.
Spreadsheets:
Creating spreadsheets using Microsoft Excel is a great computer skill to put on your resume. Spreadsheets are used widely in most office jobs and although a lot of people can input simple information, you will be a valuable candidate if you understand some of the more advanced functions.
PowerPoint:
Microsoft PowerPoint is the standard for presentations in the business world and many employers consider it one of the basic computer skills that everybody should have. If you are able to demonstrate your presentation skills during an interview, this will boost your chances of getting hired.
Job Specific Computer Skills:
As well as the basic computer skills that everybody is expected to have, there will be job specific computer skills that you need, and these will depend on the kind of field engineer job that you are looking for. For example, if you are looking for a data services position, you will need advanced hardware skills and a knowledge of connected computers to networks. You will also need expertise in fixing broken hardware. If you are looking for a network security services position, a working knowledge of firewall software is vital. These job specific skills are the ones that you should highlight the most on your resume.
